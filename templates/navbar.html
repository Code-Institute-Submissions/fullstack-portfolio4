ABOUT US


More Vegans is a Vegan fastfood chain that was founded in sweden year 2020. The purpose was, and still is, to deliver fastfood that is healthy. Due to the stressful culture we live in - alot of people (young and old) prefers to have a quick bite during their stressful day, filling them up with energy - to cope with that... More Vegans will fullfill their needs. The ingridients in our food is detailed picked to fullfill a fabolous taste, minimized unhealthy fat, maximized with immune boosting ingridients and NON chemicals (e-number carriers).
